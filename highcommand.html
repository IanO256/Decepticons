Starscream

The ambitious right hand man of Megatron. Although slightly cowardly in nature Starscream's goal is to one day rule the Decepticons
as their king. His military tactics and ability to lead is what make him useful to Megatron. He leads the seekers a group tasked with
locating Energon, and dominating the battlefield with air superiority. Giving the Decepticons a useful advantage in the sky.

Soundwave

The head of the Decepticon Intelegence division. His loyalty is unquestionable. Very few can stand up to him and his cassettes/minicons.
As he can launch a small army out of his chest if the situation ever requires it. He intercepts Autobot communications, decodes, or encrypts
information, master of disguise and inflitration. Unrivaled in his ability to obtain necesary information for the Decepticons.

Shockwave

The Mad Scientist. After having his hands and face removed by the Old Guard's secret police in act known as Empurata. He decided to take
the act one step further by purging any and all emotions from his systems. Running on pure logic. In doing so has allowed him achieve more
technological advancements for the Decepticons.
