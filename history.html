Decepticon History

D-16

It is hard to talk about the Decepticon movement without the Cybertronian who started it. D-16 was brought online to be a miner for Energon. The fuel source
for everything on Cybertron. Despite his condition D-16 made himself well educated on various topics such as politics and history. However over time the Old
Guard called for the operations in the mines to be halted and the miners to be decommissioned or transfered. Many miners opposed this and began to rebel
including D-16 who landed himself in jail during one of the protests. He managed to escape and fled to the criminal underworld of Kaon.

D-16 began to fight in illegal underground Gladitorial Arenas in the criminal underworld. It was here where he renamed himself Megatronus after one of the
13 original Primes. Yet the crowd would shorten it to Megatron. Megatron would then begin to amass a following of those who thought similar to him. That the
Old Guard must be overthrown to ensure peace and prosperity for every Cybertronian. Through a cunning and shocking move Megatron gathered his forces demonized
by the Old Guard as "Decepticons" and took Kaon from the Old Guard in one night. Killing Sentinel Prime and establishing the Decepticons their new Capital
City. From then on the Decepticons rallied more to their cause.
