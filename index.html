Decepticons

Purpose

Born on Cybertron and forced into an unjust system. This system forced Cybertronians into jobs at birth giving no opportunity for freedom. It was only
a matter of time before someone decedided to take a stand. One group of Cybertronians that decided Transform and rise up against the Old Guard were
the Decepticons. Whose intentions started off as noble to revolt against the oppression of the Old Guard. Creating Pride, Ambition, and Loyalty that
later blinded them from seeing the truth of their actions. That the Autobots more than ever fought for the freedom the Decepticons believe in.

Early Beginnings

Shortly after the battle for Kaon began, Sentinel Prime (a Prime only in title) along with his forces were deployed to Kaon to put down the Revolution.
It was here that Megatron put a stop to the Prime. And once the dust settled, the Old Guard found their leader or what was left of him. It was there
that the commanding officer decided to abandon Kaon, and conceded it to the Decepticons. Following this the Decepticons would go on to conquor more
cities their goal to conquor all of Cybertron. Eventually the Old Guard would fall. It's remenants either created the Autobots or joining the
Decepticons.
